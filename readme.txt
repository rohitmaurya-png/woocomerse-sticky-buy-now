== Description ==
 

Welcome to the Simple Sticky Buy Now For WooCommerce plugin. Use this plugin to show visitors a sticky Buy Now button on your product pages. This plugin enhances the user experience on your Woocommerce store, to help grab more sales and improve your ecommerce conversions. The plugin supports Ajax Buy Now functionality, with automatic color and font customizations according to your theme. Many top ecommerce stores are now using sticky Buy Now buttons like this (which float through the product page) to always show your potential customers the add-to-cart button. it keeps the Buy Now button in front of your customers eyes and keeps encouraging them to make a purchase. 

Some products have long content. When you finish the reading often you find add the cart button getting hidden and customers end up ignoring the Buy Now button. but if you have the Simple Sticky Buy Now it will not let the Buy Now button hidden.

 It helps to increase the conversion rate.
 Mostly  People use mobile for shopping this plugin helps visitors to add a product to cart .
